# Name Brainstorming for Aztec Liquid Staking Protocol

**Date:** December 2025  
**Purpose:** Generate a comprehensive list of potential names for the liquid staking protocol on Aztec Network, organized by theme and inspiration source.

---

## Naming Criteria

1. **Memorable** - Easy to say, spell, and remember
2. **Brandable** - Works well as a domain, social handles, and logo
3. **Relevant** - Connects to Aztec/Mexican culture OR liquid staking concept
4. **Available** - Domain and social handles should be checkable
5. **Professional** - Suitable for institutional investors and DeFi users
6. **Distinctive** - Stands out from competitors (Olla, etc.)
