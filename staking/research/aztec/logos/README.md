# Logo Mockups for Aztec Liquid Staking Protocol

**Date:** December 2025  
**Purpose:** Visual identity concepts for validated name candidates

---

## Design Principles

1. **Aztec/Mexican Cultural Elements** - Respectful incorporation of Aztec symbols
2. **Liquidity/Flow Concepts** - Visual representation of liquid staking
3. **Modern & Professional** - Suitable for DeFi/institutional use
4. **Scalable** - Works at small and large sizes
5. **Color Palette** - Consider Aztec colors (turquoise, gold, red, black)

---

## Logo Concepts

### 1. AztecYield
- **Concept:** Yield symbol + Aztec geometric patterns
- **Colors:** Turquoise (#00CED1) + Gold (#FFD700)
- **Style:** Modern, clean, professional

### 2. QuetzalStake
- **Concept:** Feathered Serpent (Quetzalcoatl) + staking symbol
- **Colors:** Green (#228B22) + Gold (#FFD700)
- **Style:** Elegant, mythological, distinctive

### 3. OllinStake
- **Concept:** Movement symbol (Ollin) + circular staking pattern
- **Colors:** Red (#DC143C) + Black (#000000)
- **Style:** Dynamic, geometric, Aztec-inspired

### 4. CenoteStake
- **Concept:** Sacred well (cenote) + liquid flow
- **Colors:** Blue (#0066CC) + Turquoise (#00CED1)
- **Style:** Fluid, natural, water-themed

### 5. AztecFlow
- **Concept:** Flow lines + Aztec geometric patterns
- **Colors:** Turquoise (#00CED1) + Gold (#FFD700)
- **Style:** Modern, dynamic, liquidity-focused
