## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Risk:** Protocol issues on launch
- **Mitigation:** Extensive testnet testing, monitoring, rapid response team

### Marketing Risks
- **Risk:** Low awareness, poor launch
- **Mitigation:** Pre-launch awareness campaign, influencer partnerships

### Competitive Risks
- **Risk:** Competitor launches first
- **Mitigation:** Speed to market, first-mover messaging

### Operational Risks
- **Risk:** Support overwhelmed
- **Mitigation:** Support documentation, FAQ, community moderators
