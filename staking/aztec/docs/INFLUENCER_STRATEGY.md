### Phase 2: Initial Outreach (Week 2-3)

**Approach:**
1. **Personalized DMs** - Not generic pitches
2. **Value First** - Offer early access, not just payment
3. **Educational** - Provide resources, not just promotion
4. **Authentic** - Build relationships, not transactions

## Risk Management (FTC Compliance)

### Disclosure Requirements

**All sponsored content must:**
- Include clear disclosure (#ad, #sponsored, #paid)
- Be transparent about relationship
- Not make false or unverified claims
- Comply with FTC guidelines

**Template Disclosures:**
- Twitter: "This post is sponsored by stAZTEC #ad"
- YouTube: "This video is sponsored by stAZTEC" (in description and video)
- Blog: "This post contains sponsored content from stAZTEC"
