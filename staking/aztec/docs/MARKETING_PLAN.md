**Created:** December 30, 2025

## Brand Positioning

### Tagline
**"Liquid staking for Aztec. Earn rewards while staying liquid."**

### Key Differentiators
1. **Privacy-Native:** Built 100% on Aztec (privacy-focused L2), not adapted from Ethereum
2. **First-Mover:** First production-ready liquid staking protocol on Aztec Network
3. **Zero Capital Requirements:** Users provide 100% of staking capital, no protocol capital needed
4. **Self-Operated Validators:** Vertical integration ensures reliability and control

### Target Audience

**Primary:**
- AZTEC token holders who want to stake but need liquidity
- DeFi users on Aztec Network seeking yield opportunities
- Privacy-focused crypto users interested in Aztec ecosystem

**Secondary:**
- Ethereum liquid staking users (stETH, rETH holders) exploring Aztec
- Institutional stakers looking for Aztec exposure
- Aztec ecosystem developers building on stAZTEC

## Messaging Framework

| Audience | Pain Point | Our Solution | Proof Point |
|----------|------------|--------------|-------------|
| **AZTEC Holders** | 200k AZTEC minimum to stake (~$8k), tokens locked | Deposit any amount, receive liquid stAZTEC | No minimum, instant liquidity |

| **Privacy Users** | Most LSTs are on transparent chains | Built on Aztec (privacy-native) | Privacy-first architecture |
| **Yield Seekers** | Current 166% APY will normalize, want steady returns | 8% APY steady-state, predictable | Sustainable yield model |

## Influencer/KOL Strategy

### Target Influencer Profiles

**Tier 1 (Aztec Ecosystem):**
- Aztec Foundation team members
- Aztec ecosystem developers
- Privacy/ZK researchers

**Tier 2 (DeFi/Liquid Staking):**
- Liquid staking thought leaders (Lido, Rocket Pool community)
- DeFi educators
- Crypto analysts

**Tier 3 (General Crypto):**
- Crypto influencers with privacy focus
- DeFi YouTubers/Podcasters
- Crypto journalists

### Outreach Approach
1. **Personalized DMs** - Explain protocol, offer early access
2. **Educational Content** - Provide resources, not just promotion
3. **Partnership Opportunities** - Co-create content, not just paid promotion

### Compensation Structures
- **Early Access:** Testnet access, early mainnet access
- **Content Creation:** Sponsored posts, video reviews
- **Ambassador Program:** Long-term relationship, token incentives (post-TGE)

### Risk Management (FTC Compliance)
- All sponsored content clearly marked (#ad, #sponsored)
- Disclosures in all paid promotions
- No false claims or unverified statistics
