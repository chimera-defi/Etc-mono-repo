## Partnership Strategy Overview

**Goal:** Integrate stAZTEC into Aztec DeFi ecosystem to make it truly "liquid"

**Approach:** Quality over quantity. Focus on high-impact integrations that drive TVL and user adoption.

**Timeline:**
- **Pre-Launch:** 1-2 partnerships secured
- **Launch:** 1+ integration live
- **Month 1:** 2+ integrations live
- **Month 3:** 5+ integrations live

## Negotiation Guidance

### Key Negotiation Points

**Liquidity Incentives:**
- Determine if liquidity mining/incentives needed
- Budget: $10k-$50k for launch liquidity
- Structure: Points, tokens (post-TGE), or direct incentives

**Co-Marketing:**
- Joint announcements
- Social media coordination
- Blog posts and content
- Event partnerships

**Technical Support:**
- Integration timeline
- Support during integration
- Documentation requirements
- Testing support

**Exclusivity:**
- Avoid exclusivity clauses (we need multiple integrations)
- Non-exclusive partnerships preferred
- First-mover benefits instead of exclusivity

## Risk Mitigation

### Integration Risks
- **Risk:** Integration delays
- **Mitigation:** Clear timelines, regular check-ins, buffer time

### Technical Risks
- **Risk:** Integration issues
- **Mitigation:** Extensive testing, technical support, documentation

### Marketing Risks
- **Risk:** Poor co-marketing execution
- **Mitigation:** Clear marketing plan, regular coordination, shared assets
