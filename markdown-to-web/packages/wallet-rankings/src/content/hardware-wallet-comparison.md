## Why Look Beyond Ledger?

**Ledger Recover (May 2023):** Firmware can extract and transmit seed phrase fragments to third-party custodians.

- ğŸ”´ **Firmware CAN extract seed** â€” attack surface exists even if "optional"

- ğŸ”´ **2020 data breach** â€” exposed 272K users to phishing and physical threats

**If you must use Ledger:** Always enable passphrase (25th word) â€” Recover cannot extract this.

### Migration Path

| From Ledger | To | Why |
|-------------|-----|-----|
| Nano S/S+ | Trezor Safe 3 | Same price, fully open source |
| Nano X | Trezor Safe 5 or Keystone 3 Pro | Better transparency or air-gapped |
