## Go / No-Go Scorecard (SpecForge)

Score each item 0-2. Proceed only if total >= 14/18 and no critical 0 in retention, reliability, or spec quality.

1. Workflow Adoption
- 0: low repeated usage
- 1: moderate pilot usage
- 2: strong repeated usage

2. Patch Trust Signal
- 0: very low acceptance or trust limited to trivial edits
- 1: moderate acceptance with mixed reviewer confidence
- 2: strong acceptance across meaningful patch classes with positive sentiment

3. Retention
- 0: teams churn after initial draft
- 1: partial return behavior
- 2: consistent weekly active usage

4. Throughput Improvement
- 0: no meaningful speed improvement
- 1: modest improvement
- 2: clear spec-to-first-commit acceleration

5. Spec Quality Improvement
- 0: no meaningful reduction in ambiguity or rework
- 1: partial quality gain
- 2: clear reduction in downstream rework or ambiguity

6. Collaboration Reliability
- 0: frequent conflict/data-loss incidents
- 1: manageable issues
- 2: stable collaboration and rollback confidence

7. Differentiation Clarity
- 0: seen as interchangeable with incumbents
- 1: partial differentiation
- 2: clear human+agent canvas and depth-gate differentiation

8. GTM Efficiency
- 0: no repeatable early channel
- 1: uncertain channel fit
- 2: repeatable acquisition channel with acceptable CAC

9. Team Readiness
- 0: missing critical capability
- 1: partial readiness
- 2: roadmap-ready for MVP execution
