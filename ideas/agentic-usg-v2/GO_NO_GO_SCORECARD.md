# Go / No-Go Scorecard

| Dimension | Score (1-5) | Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Hackathon novelty | 5 | Strong cross-over of stablecoin, AI agent, and prediction markets |
| Prize fit | 5 | Direct mapping to user-provided World, 0G, Arc tracks |
| Demo clarity | 3 | Good if narrowed; weak if too many mechanics are shown at once |
| Technical feasibility in 36h | 3 | Achievable only with mocks and ruthless scoping |
| Long-term protocol credibility | 2 | Current framing is hackathon-first and somewhat theatrical |
| Judge memorability | 4 | Prediction-yield + human-backed agent is sticky |
| Risk of overclaiming | 4 | Needs careful wording around peg defense and autonomy |

## Recommendation

Go, but only if the scope is cut to:
1. one stablecoin engine
2. one tranche display
3. one prediction-yield showcase
4. one human-gated agent action loop

## No-Go Trigger

If the team insists on live-everything, leverage loops, tokenomics, World, 0G, Arc, and multiple yield venues in the first build, the idea becomes a no-go due to narrative and execution sprawl.
