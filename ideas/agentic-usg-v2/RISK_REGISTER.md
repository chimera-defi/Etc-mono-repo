# Risk Register

| Risk | Severity | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|------------|
| Too many prize tracks creates a muddled product story | High | High | Pick one hero narrative and treat others as adapters |
| Prediction-market arb looks fake or cherry-picked | High | Medium | Use explicit fixture data or clearly labeled live scenario |
| Peg defense implies false stability guarantees | High | Medium | Frame as demo policy engine, not production-ready monetary system |
| Agent autonomy feels unsafe | High | Medium | Require human-backed proof for sensitive actions and show action audit trail |
| Tokenomics read as pure ponzinomics without substance | Medium | High | Downscope `$AGNT` to incentive flywheel, not main value prop |
| Hackathon time runs out before integrations land | High | High | Build a strong local-first demo with prize wrappers last |
| Oracle or prediction resolution trust assumptions are weak | Medium | Medium | Surface trust assumptions in UI and docs |
| Leverage loops create too much complexity | Medium | Medium | Make loops optional or simulated in MVP |
