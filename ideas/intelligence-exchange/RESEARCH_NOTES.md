### Working Observations
1. Direct credit transfer/resale constraints are a significant design boundary.
2. Routing and raw capacity markets already exist, so differentiation must be workflow- and trust-based.
3. Payment rail expansion should be phased to avoid delaying core execution validation.
4. Marketplace trust likely depends on transparent settlement + dispute traceability, not price alone.
