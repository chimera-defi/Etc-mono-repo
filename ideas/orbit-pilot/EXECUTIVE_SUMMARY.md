## Orbit Pilot Executive Summary

### One-Liner
Orbit Pilot is a launch distribution system for publishing and submitting one product launch across directories, profiles, communities, and content surfaces without collapsing into spam or policy-unsafe automation.

### Why It Matters
1. Launch teams waste hours on repeated manual submissions.
2. Inconsistent copy and links reduce attribution and credibility.
3. Browser automation is often risky, but teams still need a structured fallback strategy.

### Wedge
- launch profile once, reuse everywhere
- platform registry with policy-aware modes
- manual-first compliance posture
- official API integrations where real
- build-ready execution path for agentic workflows

### MVP
1. Canonical launch profile.
2. Seed platform registry.
3. Unique per-platform content generation.
4. UTM and asset processing.
5. Manual queue plus a few official APIs.

### Biggest Risks
1. Directory quality varies wildly.
2. Write access is uneven and sometimes unstable.
3. Teams may want reckless automation that should remain out of scope.

### Decision Rule
Proceed if teams consistently save time, improve tracking quality, and reduce submission error rates without taking on unacceptable platform risk.
