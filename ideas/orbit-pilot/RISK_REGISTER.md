## Orbit Pilot Risk Register

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Owner | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Platform policies change quickly | High | High | Product | manual-first default and registry review cadence |
| Users demand unsafe automation | High | High | Product | explicit non-goals and gated browser fallback |
| Official write access is restricted or paid | Medium | High | Eng | small official set, graceful manual fallback |
| Duplicate or repetitive copy causes moderation issues | Medium | High | Content | enforced variation and cooldown logic |
| Directory ROI is lower than expected | Medium | Medium | GTM | track referral and indexing impact per platform |
| Asset formatting breaks on niche sites | Medium | Medium | Eng | image presets and per-site metadata |
| Secrets are mishandled | Low | Critical | Eng | OS keychain and no plaintext secret storage |
