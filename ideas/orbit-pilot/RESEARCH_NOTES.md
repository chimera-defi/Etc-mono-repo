## Orbit Pilot Research Notes

### Key Findings
1. Official write access exists for some platforms, but not enough to justify an API-only product.
2. Medium in 2026 still supports existing integration tokens but does not issue new ones.
3. Crunchbase, Hacker News, and many niche directories are best treated as manual workflows.
4. Browser automation is a risky edge feature, not the product core.

### Product Positioning Conclusion
Orbit Pilot should be sold as launch ops infrastructure, not a social autoposter and not a scraping bot.
