# Go / No-Go Scorecard

| Dimension | Score (1-5) | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| Novelty | 4 | strong if fault domains are made legible |
| Realism | 3 | depends on not overstating stability |
| UX clarity | 3 | difficult but fixable |

- the idea is pitched as cross-protocol tranche vaults over a risky substrate
