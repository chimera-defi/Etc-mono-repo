# Financial Model

## Yield Sources

1. scrvUSD carry on stable side
2. trading fees or base pool yield on hybrid side
3. YB emissions
4. optional veYB-linked fee share

## Economic Truth

- base carry is the healthiest yield source
- emissions are bonus carry, not durable core carry
- lock premium is only legitimate if the locked side absorbs real exit and governance risk

## Product Split

- senior tranche shares
  - lower yield
  - lower first-loss exposure
  - shorter lock
  - better redemption rights
- junior tranche shares
  - higher yield
  - longer lock
  - absorbs more TRD, emissions, and queue risk
- ve-junior tranche shares
  - only sensible if fee share and governance exposure are explicit
