# Validation Plan

1. Can a user explain what the senior tranche is protected from and what it is not?
2. Can a user explain why the junior tranche earns more and locks longer?
3. Can a reviewer point to exactly where TRD risk appears in the UI?
4. Can a reviewer distinguish base carry from YB emissions and ve-lock premium?
5. Can a skeptic find the redemption, queue-ordering, and cap constraints quickly?

Kill condition:
- if the product still sounds like “stablecoin wrapper with boosted APY” after review, the design is not honest enough yet.
