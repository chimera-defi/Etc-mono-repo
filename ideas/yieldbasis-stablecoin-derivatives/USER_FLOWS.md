# User Flows

## Flow 1: Choose Tranche
1. Compare senior and junior tranche vault
2. Review yield split, risk split, and lock / exit order
3. Mint chosen tranche

## Flow 2: Inspect Risk
1. Open tranche dashboard
2. Review TRD, cap, emissions, lock state, and protocol allocation
3. See what affects redemption

## Flow 3: Redeem
1. Request redemption
2. See queue position and haircut risk
3. Claim when eligible
