## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best crypto credit card in 2026?

**EtherFi Cash** (92/100) is the current top card in this table because it combines self-custody, global reach, zero annual fee, and a strong rewards profile. For EU/UK users who want a simpler regional option, **Ready Card** (83) remains a strong alternative.

### Which crypto cards are non-custodial?

Current self-custodial options in this table include **EtherFi Cash** (92), **Amp Pay Black Card** (84), **Ready Card** (83), **Kast** (82), **Tria** (82), **MetaMask Card** (81), **Gnosis Pay** (79), **1inch Card** (76), **ThorWallet** (78), **Payy** (77), and several regional cards like **Solflare** and **Tuyo**.

### What is the best crypto card for the US?

For US users, **EtherFi Cash** is the strongest current option. If you are comfortable with custody on a centralized platform, **Fold**, **Gemini**, and **Coinbase Card** are still relevant depending on whether you prioritize Bitcoin rewards, credit-building, or exchange convenience.

### What is the best crypto card for business/corporate use?

**EtherFi Cash** is still the cleanest business-ready option in the table. **Reap** is the more explicitly business-focused spending product, while **Revolut Crypto** and **Crypto.com** are practical if you already operate inside those ecosystems.

### Should I use a custodial or non-custodial crypto card?

If you can get the same economics, non-custodial is the safer default because it reduces exchange and account-freeze risk. Custodial cards can still be worth it when the rewards, credit underwriting, or local availability are meaningfully better for your use case.

### What happened to Crypto.com Card?

It still exists and now ranks much higher than it used to in this project because the score is generated mechanically from the visible columns. That said, you still need to evaluate the real tier requirements, reward lockups, and exchange-custody tradeoffs before treating the headline cashback as “free.”

### Which crypto cards work globally?

The strongest global or near-global entries in the current table are **EtherFi Cash**, **Amp Pay Black Card**, **Crypto.com Visa**, **Kast**, **Tria**, **KuCard**, and **MetaMask Card**. Exact launch countries still change often, so always verify against the provider before applying.

### What are the highest cashback crypto cards?

On headline percentages, **Hi Card**, **Crypto.com Visa**, **Tria**, **Amp Pay**, and **Coinbase Card** have the biggest ceilings. In practice, the better comparison is effective rewards after tiers, regional limits, and custody tradeoffs, which is why **EtherFi Cash** still leads overall.

### Do crypto cards charge foreign exchange fees?

Many do. Several newer self-custody or regional programs advertise `0%` FX, but others still charge around `1-3%` or hide friction in the conversion path. Check the `FX Fee` column and the provider’s live card terms before deciding.

### Can I get a crypto card without KYC?

No mainstream regulated card in this table is truly no-KYC. Even self-custodial programs generally require identity checks because the card rails and issuing banks do. Treat “no-KYC crypto card” marketing with extreme skepticism.
