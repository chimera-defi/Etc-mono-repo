## Detailed Card Information

### Active Cards

#### Coinbase Card
- **Type:** Debit card
- **Cash Back:** 1% base, up to 4% with Coinbase One subscription
- **Rewards:** BTC, ETH, and other supported cryptocurrencies
- **Annual Fee:** $0
- **Foreign Transaction Fee:** 0%
- **Staking Required:** None
- **Availability:** US only
- **Notes:** Instant crypto rewards, no credit check

#### Crypto.com Visa Card
- **Type:** Debit card (prepaid)
- **Cash Back:** Tiered 0.5% to 8% based on CRO stake
  - Midnight Blue: 0.5% (no stake)

  - Royal Indigo/Jade Green: 3% ($4,000 CRO stake)
  - Frosted Rose Gold/Icy White: 5% ($40,000 CRO stake)

- **Annual Fee:** $0
- **Foreign Transaction Fee:** 0%

- **Availability:** Global (varies by region)
- **Notes:** 6-month staking lock, additional perks at higher tiers
