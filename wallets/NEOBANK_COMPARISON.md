### detailed Breakdown

#### 1. Juno (US)
*   **Best For:** Crypto-native individuals who want to live on-chain.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **Zero Coin Architecture:** Direct on-ramp to Layer 2s (Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base).
    *   **Paycheck in Crypto:** Automatically convert a % of direct deposit to BTC/ETH/USDC.
    *   **Partner:** Evolve Bank & Trust (FDIC Insured up to $250k).
*   **Policy:** Built specifically for crypto users. No blocks on exchanges.

#### 2. Revolut (Global)
*   **Best For:** Travelers and users who want "one app for everything".
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **Exchange:** Built-in crypto exchange with 100+ tokens.
    *   **Staking:** Earn rewards on supported assets (DOT, ETH, etc.).
    *   **Spend:** Spend crypto directly with the Revolut card (auto-conversion).
*   **Policy:** Crypto is a core product. External withdrawals supported (with limits).

#### 3. Xapo Bank (Global / Gibraltar)
*   **Best For:** High Net Worth Individuals (HNWI) seeking jurisdiction diversification.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **License:** Fully licensed bank in Gibraltar (not just a fintech wrapper).
    *   **Interest:** Earns interest on both USD and Bitcoin deposits.
    *   **Lightning:** Supports Lightning Network for instant BTC payments.
*   **Policy:** Bitcoin-native. Strict KYC/AML but fully integrated.

### Detailed Breakdown

#### 1. Mercury (US)
*   **Best For:** Venture-backed startups, DAOs (structured as LLCs/C-Corps), and SaaS companies.
*   **Policy:**
    *   **Acceptable:** sending/receiving wires to Coinbase/Kraken/Circle.
    *   **Restricted:** Money Service Businesses (MSBs), direct token issuers, or gambling.
    *   **Treasury:** Offers "Mercury Treasury" for yield on idle cash (T-Bills), critical for startups.
*   **Why it wins:** Great UI, free wires, easy integration with accounting software.

#### 2. FV Bank (Puerto Rico / US Licensed)
*   **Best For:** Funds, Exchanges, and companies that *need* to hold crypto and fiat in one place.
*   **Policy:** expressly licensed to provide custody services for digital assets.
*   **Features:** Integrated custody and settlement.
