## Quick Summary

**Category:** Stablecoin Payment Networks  
**Definition:** Mobile-first P2P payment networks that enable instant QR code payments using stablecoins, with bank withdrawal capabilities. Similar to UPI (India) but crypto-based.

**Key Differentiator:** QR code payments + stablecoins + bank withdrawal (off-ramp)

**Example:** Peanut (Argentina) - QR code payments with USDC/USDT, bank withdrawal to ARS
