## Executive Summary

Neo-banks with crypto features represent a bridge between traditional banking and cryptocurrency. They offer fiat accounts with integrated crypto trading, storage, and spending capabilities. This research identifies 25+ crypto-friendly neo-banks and proposes a comparison framework.

**Key Findings:**
- **25+ neo-banks identified** (mix of traditional neo-banks with crypto features and crypto-first banking services)
- **Data availability:** Good (public websites, app stores, regulatory databases)
- **Scoring focus:** Crypto features, fees, geographic availability, regulatory status
- **Integration complexity:** Medium (different from wallets but complementary)

**Recommendation:** ‚úÖ **Medium Priority** - Neo-banks complement wallets but serve different use cases (banking vs. wallet management).

## Key Differentiators

### Neo-Banks vs. Wallets
- **Neo-banks:** Fiat accounts, banking features, debit cards, regulatory compliance
- **Wallets:** Crypto storage, DeFi access, self-custody focus

### Neo-Banks vs. Exchanges
- **Neo-banks:** Banking services, debit cards, spending capabilities
- **Exchanges:** Trading focus, advanced order types, derivatives

### Neo-Banks vs. On/Off-Ramps
- **Neo-banks:** Full banking accounts, ongoing relationship
- **On/Off-Ramps:** One-time conversion services, no account needed
