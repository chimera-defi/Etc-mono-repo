## Detailed Card Reviews

### Coinbase Card

**Overview:**
The Coinbase Card is a debit card that allows users to spend crypto and earn crypto rewards. It's linked directly to your Coinbase account.

**Key Features:**
- Instant crypto rewards (no waiting for statement)
- No credit check required
- Supports multiple cryptocurrencies
- US-only availability

**Rewards Structure:**
- Base: 1% cash back in crypto
- Coinbase One subscribers: Up to 4% cash back
- Rewards paid in BTC, ETH, or other supported cryptos

**Fees:**
- Annual Fee: $0
- Foreign Transaction Fee: 0%
- ATM Fee: Standard ATM fees may apply
- Crypto Conversion: Spread fees apply

**Requirements:**
- Coinbase account
- US residency
- KYC verification

**Best For:**
- Coinbase users who want simple crypto rewards
- Users who prefer debit cards over credit cards
- Those who want instant rewards

**Limitations:**
- US only
- Requires Coinbase account
- Debit card (no credit building)

---

### Crypto.com Visa Card

**Overview:**
The Crypto.com Visa Card is a prepaid debit card with tiered rewards based on CRO token staking. Higher staking tiers unlock better rewards and perks.

**Key Features:**
- Tiered reward system (0.5% to 8%)
- Additional perks at higher tiers (Netflix, Spotify, etc.)
- Global availability (varies by region)
- Prepaid debit card

**Rewards Structure:**
- **Midnight Blue:** 0.5% cash back (no stake required)
- **Ruby Steel:** 2% cash back ($400 CRO stake)
- **Royal Indigo/Jade Green:** 3% cash back ($4,000 CRO stake)
- **Frosted Rose Gold/Icy White:** 5% cash back ($40,000 CRO stake)
- **Obsidian:** 8% cash back ($400,000 CRO stake)

**Additional Perks (Higher Tiers):**
- Netflix/Spotify rebates
- Airport lounge access
- Higher ATM withdrawal limits
- Crypto.com Exchange fee discounts

**Fees:**
- Annual Fee: $0
- Foreign Transaction Fee: 0%
- Staking Lock: 6 months (for staking tiers)

**Requirements:**
- Crypto.com account
- CRO staking for higher tiers (optional for base tier)
- KYC verification

**Best For:**
- Users willing to stake CRO for better rewards
- Those who want additional perks (Netflix, Spotify, etc.)
- Global users (availability varies)

**Limitations:**
- 6-month staking lock for tier benefits
- CRO token value volatility risk
- Prepaid card (no credit building)

## Security & Trust

### Considerations

1. **Regulatory Compliance:** Verify cards are licensed in your jurisdiction
2. **Insurance:** Check if funds are insured (FDIC, etc.)
3. **Company Track Record:** Research company history and security incidents
4. **Token Volatility:** Staking requirements expose you to token price risk
5. **Lock-up Periods:** Crypto.com requires 6-month staking lock

### Trust Indicators

- **Established Companies:** Coinbase, Gemini, Binance, Crypto.com
- **Regulatory Status:** Check licensing in your jurisdiction
- **Security Audits:** Research if cards/platforms have been audited
- **User Reviews:** Check community feedback and reviews
