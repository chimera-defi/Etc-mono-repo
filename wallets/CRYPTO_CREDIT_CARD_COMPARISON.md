## Comparison Metrics

### Key Metrics to Compare

1. **Cash Back Rates**
   - Base cash back percentage
   - Tiered rewards (if applicable)
   - Crypto-specific bonus rates

2. **Fees**
   - Annual fee
   - Foreign transaction fees
   - ATM fees
   - Monthly maintenance fees
   - Crypto conversion fees

3. **Rewards Structure**
   - Reward currency (crypto vs. fiat)
   - Reward caps/limits
   - Redemption options
   - Reward expiration

4. **Eligibility & Requirements**
   - Credit score requirements
   - Minimum staking/balance requirements
   - Geographic availability
   - KYC/verification requirements

5. **Crypto Features**
   - Supported cryptocurrencies
   - Staking requirements
   - Crypto-to-fiat conversion
   - Crypto spending options

6. **Card Type**
   - Credit card vs. debit card
   - Prepaid vs. traditional credit
   - Crypto-backed vs. crypto rewards

## Provider/Exchange Scoring Handicaps

To ensure consistent scoring, cards from the same category receive identical handicaps:

| Category | Handicap | Rationale |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| **Exchange-Based Cards** | -3 pts | Custody risk, platform dependency, potential for exchange issues |
| **DeFi-Native Cards** | +0 pts | Non-custodial, but higher technical complexity |
| **Fintech/Standalone** | +0 pts | Independent providers, varies by company |
| **Crypto.com Specific** | -20 pts | Poor UX, customer service issues, staking lock-ups, company reputation |
| **404/Verification Issues** | -5 pts | Unclear card status, potential discontinuation |
