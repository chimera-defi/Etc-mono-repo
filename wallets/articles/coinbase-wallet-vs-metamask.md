## Key Differences

### 1) Ecosystem
- **MetaMask:** Broadest dApp compatibility.
- **Coinbase:** Tight Coinbase integration (Base and Coinbase services).

### 2) Account Features
- **Coinbase:** Smart wallet features (EIP-4337).
- **MetaMask:** EOA-first, with newer features rolling out.

### 3) Release Cadence
- **MetaMask:** Higher churn (~8 rel/mo).
- **Coinbase:** Lower churn (~2 rel/mo).

---

## Pros & Cons

**Coinbase Wallet**
- ✅ Coinbase ecosystem integration
- ✅ Account abstraction support
- ⚠️ Ecosystem smaller than MetaMask

**MetaMask**
- ✅ Widest compatibility
- ✅ Most integrations
- ❌ Higher release churn

---

## Recommendations

- **Primary wallet for compatibility:** MetaMask  
- **If you want AA + Coinbase integration:** Coinbase Wallet

---

## FAQ

**Which is better for developers?**  
Use MetaMask for compatibility testing, Coinbase for AA experiments.

**Which is more stable?**  
Coinbase Wallet tends to update less frequently.
