## Pros & Cons

**Non-custodial cards** (EtherFi, Ready, Gnosis Pay)  
- ✅ You control funds  
- ✅ Lower exchange risk  
- ⚠️ Availability depends on region

**Custodial cards** (Coinbase, Gemini)  
- ✅ Simple onboarding  
- ❌ Exchange custody risk
