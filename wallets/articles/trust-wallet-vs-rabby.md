## Key Differences

### 1) Developer Tooling
- **Rabby:** Transaction simulation + scam detection.
- **Trust:** No built-in simulation.

### 2) Multi-Chain Coverage
- **Trust:** Broad chain support (EVM + non-EVM).
- **Rabby:** EVM-focused with strong developer UX.

### 3) Stability
- **Trust:** Lower release churn (~3/mo per Wallet Radar notes).
- **Rabby:** Moderate churn with strong QA.
