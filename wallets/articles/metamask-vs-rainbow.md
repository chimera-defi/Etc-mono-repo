# MetaMask vs Rainbow: Which Wallet is Better for Everyday Ethereum Use?

## Quick Answer

**Rainbow (77/100)** is better for everyday Ethereum use due to stronger UX and stability. **MetaMask (73/100)** remains essential for compatibility testing because of its dominant ecosystem.

## Key Differences

### 1) User Experience
- **Rainbow:** Clean UI and NFT-friendly experience.
- **MetaMask:** Functional but less refined UX.

### 2) Ecosystem Coverage
- **MetaMask:** Widest dApp support and integrations.
- **Rainbow:** Strong Ethereum-first focus.

### 3) Stability
- **MetaMask:** Higher release churn (~8/mo per Wallet Radar notes).
- **Rainbow:** Lower churn and better code quality signals.

---

## Pros & Cons

**Rainbow**
- ✅ Best-in-class NFT UX
- ✅ Lower churn than MetaMask
- ⚠️ Smaller ecosystem

**MetaMask**
- ✅ Universal compatibility
- ✅ Widest WalletConnect coverage
- ❌ Higher churn and maintenance burden

## Recommendations

- **Primary wallet:** Rainbow (better UX)
- **Compatibility testing:** MetaMask (required for broad coverage)
