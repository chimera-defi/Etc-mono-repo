# Positioning Brief

## Product Category

Developer-centric crypto access intelligence and comparison platform.

## Primary User

Builders, technical operators, and power users who need verified product data before integrating or adopting wallets, cards, ramps, and adjacent rails.

## Core Promise

Transparent, source-linked scoring and verification across crypto access products with reproducible methodology and public update scripts.

## Differentiator

Disclosure-first, method-driven rankings tied to visible table data and auditable refresh workflows, not sponsored listicles or opaque editorial scoring.

## Naming Constraints

- Max two words (or one fused compound made from two words)
- Clear enough for SEO intent without spammy keyword stuffing
- Should still fit if scope expands beyond custody into payments and banking access rails
- Prefer names that can survive product-line growth (wallets + ramps + cards + banks)
